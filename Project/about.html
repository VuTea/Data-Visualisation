About Us

Why Did We Choose What We Chose?

The topic of energy is the theme of this semester's data visualisation project but there is more to it than that. We also believe that in order

to combat climate change we first need to spread awareness and what better way to do that than to present our case with facts and visualisations.

Therefore, we decided to tackle the problem by highlighting the progress of green and clean energy through visualisations.
